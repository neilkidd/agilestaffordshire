There’s always a great atmosphere and discussion at Agile Staffordshire whenever we have a guest speaker. February did not disappoint. Craig Judson<\/a>\u00a0(Operations Manager at Codeweavers<\/a>)\u00a0presented Kanban in general and then elaborated on how Kanban is utilised specifically for continuous delivery of software.<\/p>\n

Subsequently, a vibrant and energised discussion took place on working practices and project\/product management. It was great to have such a varied mix of disciplines and roles present to contribute; consultants, developers, academics and managers. It was particularly interesting to note how various people were adapting well-known techniques to their work place and teams.<\/p>\n

Thank you, Craig!<\/p>\n
