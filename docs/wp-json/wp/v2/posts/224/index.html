It was nice to see a good number of people turning up for this months meeting, especially as there was no defined plan.<\/p>\n

This session was intended to be an informal open-space<\/a> type meeting, with a view to generating ideas for future sessions. The contrast between the google group<\/a> and the meeting was startling, proving that co-location, good food and beer are ideal ingredients to get a discussion going.<\/p>\n

To generate a backlog of ideas we held a productive brainstorming session, with the post-its kindly sponsored by Codeweavers<\/a>!<\/p>\n
