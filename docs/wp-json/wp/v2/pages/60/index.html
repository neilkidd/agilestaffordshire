Meetings<\/h2>\n

Currently held at Staffordshire University<\/a>, although every meeting will be documented both on the blog and on Meetup\u00a0so you can easily check the venue.<\/p>\n

What You’ll Need<\/h2>\n

Most meetings consist of a talk followed by an exercise, usually in programming.<\/p>\n

A laptop loaded with an IDE and development language of your choice is normally useful, but we often share. Any specific languages or tools will be announced prior to meetings that require them.<\/p>\n

The group has a classroom licence for JetBrains’ PHPStorm which integrates PHPUnit and JS unit testing libraries so contact Paul Shannon on paul at paulshannon.com for the licence key.<\/p>\n

You don’t need to sign up to the mailing list, or even let us know you’re coming, just turn up. You don’t even need to say hello but I’m sure you’ll want to after seeing the\u00a0jollity\u00a0of the meeting. The group is a mix of men and women from both industrial and\u00a0academic\u00a0backgrounds, we even have the odd student turn up.<\/p>\n

Don’t think that you need to be an Uber Geek<\/a>\u00a0to attend, we all started somewhere. So come along and get involved!<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
